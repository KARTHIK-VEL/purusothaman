About Profile

An AI Researcher & implementation specialist — over a decade translating advanced neural architectures into production-ready deployments on physical hardware.

Currently serving as Head of Product Development (AI) at Edsols Innovations and active Senior AI Researcher, Dr. Purusothaman has spent eleven years bridging the gap between state-of-the-art research and industrial execution — designing custom neural networks, configuring high-performance NVIDIA GPU clusters, and leading engineering teams.

As an NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute Ambassador, he has spearheaded AI workshops across more than fifteen leading institutions, deploying edge AI development kits, and bridging academic theory with practical, industry-standard machine learning workflows.
